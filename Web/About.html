About Us

One Step Ahead is Asia's largest independent publicly traded Asian life insurance group, with a presence in 18 Asian markets.

For more than a century, One Step Ahead has worked to make a significant, positive difference in the lives of our customers and communities throughout Asia. As we look ahead, our Purpose reinforces our commitment: to help millions of people live Healthier, Longer, Better Lives. Our Purpose guides our decisions and actions as an organization: to empower and enable people to understand and manage their health while meeting their long-term savings and protection needs. We believe that contributing to the creation of a healthier Asia is one of the most important and valuable things we can do for our communities, both now and in the future.

Covid-19 Safety Policy

One Step Ahead the nation's insurance provider by indemnifying the insured, is committed to complying with the Ministry of Health Guidelines, Legal and Other Regulatory Requirements to protect its employees, customers, and other stakeholders, and to continually improve the COVID 19 Safety Management System.
We provide a framework to comply with COVID L9 Safety Control Plans by establishing a Covid Task Force, effective communication, motivation, and understanding of all employees throughout the organization. We have set compliance with the COVID Safety Control Plan as our organizational goal.
